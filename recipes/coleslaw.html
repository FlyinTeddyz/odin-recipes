My coleslaw recipe

This is coleslaw I recreated after having it. Best use it for hot chicken

Ingredients

  • Dole's coleslaw mix
  • 3 tbs mayonnaise
  • 2 tbs vinegar
  • ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tbs sugar

Steps

  1. Dump contents of Dole's coleslaw mix into large bowl
  2. Add mayonnaise, vinegar, ground black pepper, and sugar
  3. Mix and leave in fidge for 30 minutes
