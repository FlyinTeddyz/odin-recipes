Pork Belly Adobo with Garlic Fried Rice

A Dish made by Disneyland I recreated

This is a dish made by Disneyland during their Festival of Holidays I recreated. Contains pork belly, garlic fried rice, green onions, and chicharrón.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork belly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 tbs brown sugar
  • black pepper
  • white rice
  • 2 heads of garlic
  • avocado oil
  • 6 cloves roasted garlic
  • 3 tbs garlic oil
  • green onion
  • chicharrón
